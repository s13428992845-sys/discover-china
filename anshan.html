Anshan

The 'Steel Capital' with world-class jade Buddhas and healing hot springs.

Home / Liaoning Province / Anshan

City Overview

Anshan is known as China's 'Steel Capital' for its massive iron and steel industry. But beyond the factories, it offers two remarkable attractions: the world's largest jade Buddha at Yushan National Park, and the Anshan Hot Springs — natural thermal waters that have been used for healing for centuries.

📍 Location

Central-southern Liaoning, 90km south of Shenyang. HSR available.

⚙️ Steel Capital

China's largest steel-producing city — a symbol of China's industrial might.

🙏 Jade Buddha

Home to the world's largest jade Buddha — a 260-ton sculpture carved from a single jade boulder.

♨️ Hot Springs

Natural hot springs with temperatures up to 70°C — believed to have healing properties.

Top Attractions

🙏 Yushan National Park

The 260-ton jade Buddha — the world's largest — is a breathtaking sight carved from a single jade mountain.

♨️ Anshan Hot Springs

Soak in natural thermal waters — the Tanggangzi area has several resort options.

🏛️ Qianshan

A scenic mountain area just outside Anshan — Buddhist temples, Taoist temples, and beautiful peaks.

⚙️ Steel Museum

Learn about China's industrial history at the Anshan Steel Museum — surprisingly fascinating.

Best Time to Visit Anshan

🌸 Spring (Mar-May)

Pleasant warming. Hot springs enjoyable in the cool spring air.

☀️ Summer (Jun-Aug)

Warm. Good for Qianshan mountain hiking. Hot springs less appealing in heat.

🍂 Autumn (Sep-Nov)

Best season. Clear skies, comfortable. Qianshan's fall colors are spectacular.

❄️ Winter (Dec-Feb)

Cold. Hot springs are most enjoyable in winter — soak outdoors in the cold air.

Anshan Travel Tips

⚠️ Important Reminders

  • Qianshan: Dedicate a full day — the mountain area has over 30 temples and scenic viewpoints.
  • Hot Springs: The best experience is an outdoor hot spring in winter — the contrast of hot water and cold air is unforgettable.
  • Combine: Anshan is 90km from Shenyang — easy to combine both cities in one trip.
  • Jade: Anshan is a major jade trading center — the jade market near Yushan is worth visiting for collectors.
