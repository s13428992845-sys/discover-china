Guilin

The gateway to China's most spectacular karst landscape — rivers, peaks, and rice terraces.

Home / Guangxi / Guilin

City Overview

Guilin's name means 'Forest of Osmanthus' — and this city lives up to its poetic name. Surrounded by dramatic limestone karst peaks and bisected by the Li River, it has been inspiring Chinese artists and poets for over a thousand years. The famous phrase 'Guilin's scenery is the finest under heaven' (桂林山水甲天下) has been quoted for 800 years.

📍 Location

Northeastern Guangxi. A major tourist destination with an international airport.

⛰️ Karst Landscape

Surreal limestone peaks rising from flat plains — a landscape that defines Chinese art.

🚢 Li River

The 83km Li River cruise to Yangshuo is one of China's most iconic journeys.

🌾 Longji Terraces

The 'Dragon's Backbone' rice terraces — 700 years of agricultural artistry carved into mountains.

Top Attractions

🚢 Li River Cruise

The classic 4-hour cruise from Guilin to Yangshuo — the most famous river scenery in China.

🌙 Elephant Trunk Hill

Guilin's symbol — a hill shaped like an elephant drinking from the river.

🏯 Reed Flute Cave

A spectacular limestone cave — illuminated stalactites and stalagmites create a magical underground world.

🌾 Longji Rice Terraces

A 2-hour drive from Guilin — spectacular terraces that change color with the seasons.

Best Time to Visit Guilin

🌸 Spring (Mar-May)

Misty and magical. Karst peaks emerge from clouds. Rice terraces flooded for planting.

☀️ Summer (Jun-Aug)

Hot but dramatic. Rivers are high for cruises. Waterfalls at peak flow.

🍂 Autumn (Sep-Nov)

BEST SEASON. Clear skies, golden harvest terraces. Perfect photography conditions.

❄️ Winter (Dec-Feb)

Cool and misty. Few tourists. Snow-dusted karst peaks are rare but spectacular.

Guilin Travel Tips

⚠️ Important Reminders

  • Li River Cruise: Book the 4-star boat for comfort. Take the downstream route (Guilin→Yangshuo) for the best scenery.
  • Longji Terraces: Best in June (water-filled mirrors) or September-October (golden harvest). Stay overnight in a village.
  • Reed Flute Cave: Visit in the morning when the cave lighting creates the most dramatic effects.
  • Night Cruise: The evening Two Rivers and Four Lakes cruise in Guilin city is beautifully illuminated.
