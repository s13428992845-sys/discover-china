Jinan

The 'Spring City' — famous artesian springs, Daming Lake, and as gateway to Mount Tai (1 hour south).

Home / Shandong Province / Jinan

City Overview

Jinan is famous as the 'City of Springs' — over 100 natural artesian springs bubble up throughout the city. Combined with Daming Lake and its location just 1 hour from Mount Tai, it's the perfect base for exploring Shandong's cultural heartland.

📍 Location

Western Shandong, at the foot of Mount Tai. 1 hour by high-speed train to the mountain.

💧 Springs

Over 100 natural artesian springs — Baotu Spring is the most famous, with water flowing for 2,500+ years.

🏞️ Daming Lake

A large spring-fed lake in the city center — surrounded by willows, lotus flowers, and pavilions.

🚄 Mount Tai Gateway

Just 1 hour by train from the base of sacred Mount Tai — the best jumping-off point.

Top Attractions

💧 Baotu Spring

The 'Number One Spring Under Heaven' — 2,500 years of continuous flow. Tea house uses spring water.

🏞️ Daming Lake

Walk the lakeside paths — willow trees, lotus in summer, and traditional pavilions around the water.

🏛️ Thousand Buddha Mountain

Hilltop park with hundreds of Buddha statues carved in the cliffs. Panoramic views of the city.

🍺 Qingdao Beer Garden

Jinan has great beer too — try local craft breweries alongside the famous Qingdao brand.

Best Time to Visit Jinan

🌸 Spring (Mar-May)

Springs flow strongest after winter rain. Comfortable temperatures. Beautiful lotus preparation.

☀️ Summer (Jun-Aug)

Lotus flowers on Daming Lake are spectacular. Hot but spring water keeps things cool.

🍂 Autumn (Sep-Nov)

Best season. Clear skies, comfortable temps. Perfect for combining with Mount Tai.

❄️ Winter (Dec-Feb)

Cold but springs flow warm (18°C year-round). Misty, atmospheric scenes at Baotu Spring.

Jinan Travel Tips

⚠️ Important Reminders

  • Spring Water Tea: Drink tea brewed with actual Baotu Spring water at the spring-side tea house — a unique experience.
  • Mount Tai Combo: Take the 1-hour high-speed train to Tai'an, climb Mount Tai overnight for sunrise.
  • Black Tiger Spring: Less touristy than Baotu — locals collect water here. More atmospheric and authentic.
  • Food Street: Furong Street snack market has excellent Shandong street food — try the sweet and sour carp.
