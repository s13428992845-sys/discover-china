Qinhuangdao (Beidaihe)

China's classic beach resort — where the Great Wall meets the sea at Shanhaiguan, plus sandy Beidaihe beaches.

Home / Hebei Province / Qinhuangdao (Beidaihe)

City Overview

Qinhuangdao is Hebei's coastal city, famous for Beidaihe beach (China's classic summer resort) and Shanhaiguan — where the Great Wall meets the Bohai Sea. It's been a retreat for Chinese leaders and a beach destination for decades.

📍 Location

Eastern Hebei, on the Bohai Sea coast. 300km east of Beijing. High-speed train connects in 2 hours.

🏯 Shanhaiguan

The 'First Pass Under Heaven' — where the Great Wall meets the sea. Eastern end of the Ming Dynasty Great Wall.

🏖️ Beidaihe

China's most famous beach resort — 20km of sandy beaches. Summer retreat for Chinese leaders since Mao's era.

🦅 Bird Watching

One of the world's top migration sites — over 400 bird species recorded. Peak migration in April-May and September.

Top Attractions

🏯 Shanhaiguan

Walk where the Great Wall meets the sea at 'Old Dragon's Head.' Explore the fortified pass and museums.

🏖️ Beidaihe Beach

Swim, sunbathe, and walk the 20km sandy shore. Fresh seafood restaurants along the waterfront.

🏛️ Jiaoshan Great Wall

A restored section of the Great Wall overlooking Shanhaiguan pass — fewer crowds, great views.

🦅 Migration Points

The coastal wetlands are world-class for birdwatching during spring and autumn migration.

Best Time to Visit Qinhuangdao (Beidaihe)

🌸 Spring (Mar-May)

Cool and breezy. World-class bird migration in April-May. Few beach visitors.

☀️ Summer (Jun-Aug)

Peak beach season. Warm (25-30°C). Very crowded during Chinese holidays. Book everything early.

🍂 Autumn (Sep-Nov)

Pleasant temperatures. Good for Great Wall visits. Bird migration returns in September.

❄️ Winter (Dec-Feb)

Cold (-10 to 0°C). Beaches are empty. Dramatic frozen sea views at Shanhaiguan.

Qinhuangdao (Beidaihe) Travel Tips

⚠️ Important Reminders

  • Shanhaiguan First: The Great Wall meeting the sea is the highlight. Allow 3-4 hours minimum.
  • Avoid Holidays: Beidaihe is packed during July-August Chinese school holidays. Visit in June or September instead.
  • Seafood: Fresh from the Bohai Sea — try clams, crab, and shrimp at the waterfront restaurants.
  • Bird Migration: If visiting in April-May or September, bring binoculars — this is a world-class birding destination.
