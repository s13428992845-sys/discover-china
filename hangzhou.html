Hangzhou

Marco Polo's 'City of Heaven' — West Lake, Longjing tea, Lingyin Temple, and China's digital economy hub.

Home / Zhejiang Province / Hangzhou

City Overview

Hangzhou has been one of China's most important cities for over a thousand years. Marco Polo called it 'the finest and noblest city in the world.' Today it blends West Lake's timeless beauty with the cutting-edge digital economy of Alibaba.

📍 Location

Northern Zhejiang, at the head of Hangzhou Bay. 1 hour from Shanghai by HSR.

🏞️ West Lake

UNESCO World Heritage — the most famous lake in China, inspiring poets and painters for 1,000+ years.

🍵 Tea Capital

Birthplace of Longjing (Dragon Well) tea — China's most famous green tea. Tea culture permeates the city.

💻 Tech Hub

Headquarters of Alibaba and a booming tech industry. China's most digital-friendly city.

Top Attractions

🏞️ West Lake

Boat rides, lotus flowers, willow-lined paths. The Ten Scenes of West Lake are a thousand-year tradition.

🏛️ Lingyin Temple

One of China's largest Buddhist temples — 1,700 years old, nestled in forested hills with ancient grottoes.

🍵 Longjing Village

Visit tea plantations, taste fresh Longjing tea, and learn about tea culture in the birthplace of China's finest green tea.

🌊 Qiantang River

The world's largest tidal bore — a dramatic natural phenomenon reaching 9 meters high in autumn.

Best Time to Visit Hangzhou

🌸 Spring (Mar-May)

Perfect. Peach and cherry blossoms around West Lake. Tea picking season at Longjing. Warm and pleasant.

☀️ Summer (Jun-Aug)

Hot and humid (35°C+). Lotus flowers on West Lake are spectacular. Escape to mountain tea villages.

🍂 Autumn (Sep-Nov)

Ideal. Cool, clear skies. Full moon over West Lake (Mid-Autumn) is legendary. Tidal bore season.

❄️ Winter (Dec-Feb)

Cold (0-8°C). 'Broken Bridge in Snow' is one of the Ten Scenes. Fewer crowds, poetic atmosphere.

Hangzhou Travel Tips

⚠️ Important Reminders

  • West Lake Circuit: Rent a bike to circle the lake (15km). Stop at Leifeng Pagoda for panoramic views.
  • Tea Experience: Visit Longjing Village in March-April for tea picking. Buy directly from farmers.
  • Lingyin Early: Arrive before 7AM to experience morning chanting and avoid tour groups.
  • Food Guide: Try Dongpo pork (braised pork belly named after poet Su Dongpo) and West Lake vinegar fish.
