Huangguoshu Waterfall

Asia's largest waterfall — a thundering 77.8-meter-high, 101-meter-wide cascade where you can walk behind the curtain of water.

Home / Guizhou / Huangguoshu

📍 Top Attractions

Huangguoshu Main Falls

The main attraction — a massive 77.8m-high waterfall. Walk through the Water Curtain Cave (水帘洞) behind the falls for a truly unique perspective.

Doupotang Waterfall (陡坡塘)

The widest waterfall in the scenic area at 105m across, famous as the filming location for the classic TV series Journey to the West.

Tianxingqiao Scenic Area (天星桥)

A stunning karst landscape with stone bridges, limestone formations, and the beautiful Silver Chain Waterfall.

🎫 Tickets

  • Peak Season (Mar-Nov): ¥160 + ¥50 shuttle bus
  • Off Season (Dec-Feb): ¥150 + ¥50 shuttle bus
  • Escalator: ¥50 round-trip (optional)

💡 Travel Tips

  • Best season: June–August when water flow is at its peak
  • Bring a raincoat — you will get wet at the Water Curtain Cave!
  • Plan 1 full day. From Guiyang: ~2 hours by high-speed rail to Anshun + 45 min bus
  • Arrive early (8 AM) to avoid crowds at the main waterfall
