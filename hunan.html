Hunan Province

Avatar's floating mountains come alive in Zhangjiajie, fiery cuisine, and the poetic beauty of Phoenix Ancient Town.

Home / Provinces / Hunan Province

Province Overview

Avatar's floating mountains come alive in Zhangjiajie, fiery cuisine, and the poetic beauty of Phoenix Ancient Town.

📍 Location

South-central China, south of Lake Dongting. Borders Hubei, Jiangxi, Guangdong, Guangxi, Guizhou, and Chongqing.

👥 Population

Approximately 66 million. Known for spicy cuisine, revolutionary history, and stunning natural landscapes.

🌡️ Climate

Subtropical monsoon. Hot summers, mild winters. Very humid. Best visited Mar-May and Sep-Nov.

🏔️ Terrain

Mountains in the west (Zhangjiajie, Fenghuang), hills in the south, and the Dongting Lake plain in the north.

🍜 Cuisine

Hunan cuisine (Xiang cuisine) — one of China's Eight Great Cuisines. Famous for intense spiciness, smoked meats, and fresh flavors.

✈️ Major Airport

Changsha Huanghua (CSX) — well-connected domestically and with some international routes.

Why Visit Hunan Province?

🏔️ Zhangjiajie

Avatar's 'Hallelujah Mountain' inspiration — thousands of sandstone pillars rising from subtropical forest. UNESCO Global Geopark.

🏘️ Fenghuang

Phoenix Ancient Town — stilted wooden houses along the Tuojiang River, Miao minority culture, and timeless atmosphere.

🏔️ Tianmen Mountain

The 'Heaven's Gate' — a natural arch at the summit, the world's longest cable car, and a cliff-hanging glass walkway.

🌊 Dongting Lake

China's second-largest freshwater lake — vast wetlands, bird sanctuaries, and the poetic 'Xiang River' connection.

🏛️ Shaoshan

Birthplace of Mao Zedong — a small town that became a major pilgrimage site in modern Chinese history.

🌿 Furong Town

A 2,000-year-old Tujia town built on a waterfall — ancient stilted buildings and spectacular night views.

Best Time to Visit

🌸 Spring (Mar-May)

Good season. Warming up, flowers blooming. Zhangjiajie's misty peaks are atmospheric. Can be rainy.

☀️ Summer (Jun-Aug)

Hot and humid (35°C+). Zhangjiajie is cooler but crowded. Fenghuang is lively with nightlife.

🍂 Autumn (Sep-Nov)

Best season. Clear skies, comfortable temperatures, and autumn colors transform Zhangjiajie's peaks.

❄️ Winter (Dec-Feb)

Mild (2-8°C) but damp. Snow on Zhangjiajie's peaks is spectacular but rare. Few tourists.

Getting Around Hunan Province

✈️ By Air

  • Main Airport: Changsha Huanghua (CSX) — major hub. Zhangjiajie Hehua (DYG) for direct access to the national park.
  • Regional: Fenghuang has a small airport opened recently.

🚄 By Train

  • High-Speed Rail: Changsha to Beijing (5.5h), Guangzhou (2.5h), Shanghai (5h), Wuhan (1.5h).
  • Zhangjiajie: HSR now connects Changsha to Zhangjiajie in 2.5 hours.

🚌 By Bus

  • Scenic Buses: Regular services from Zhangjiajie city to the national park (40 min) and Tianmen Mountain.
  • Fenghuang: Accessible by bus from Zhangjiajie (2h) or Huaihua (1h).

🚕 By Taxi

  • Availability: Taxis and Didi available in all cities. Zhangjiajie has tourist taxis.
  • Pricing: Starting at 8-10 CNY. Negotiate for longer trips.

Hunan Province Travel Tips

⚠️ Important Reminders

  • Zhangjiajie Time: Allow at least 3 days — 1 day for the national park, 1 for Tianmen Mountain, 1 for the glass bridge.
  • Fenghuang at Night: The town transforms after dark with lantern-lit stilted houses reflecting on the river. Stay overnight.
  • Spi cy Level: Hunan food is VERY spicy. Tell restaurants 'bu yao la' (no spice) if you can't handle heat.
  • Stinky Tofu: Changsha's signature street food — smell is intense but the taste is incredible. Try it at least once!
  • Avatar Views: The 'Southern Sky Column' was renamed 'Avatar Hallelujah Mountain'. Go early to avoid crowds.
  • Tianmen Tips: Take the world's longest cable car (7,455m). The glass skywalk requires shoe covers (provided).
  • Furong Town: Best at night when the waterfall-lit buildings glow. A great stop between Zhangjiajie and Fenghuang.
  • Payment: Cash rarely used. Set up Alipay or WeChat Pay before arrival.
