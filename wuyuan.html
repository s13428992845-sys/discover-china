Wuyuan

China's most beautiful countryside — ancient Hui-style villages, rapeseed flower fields, and autumn foliage.

Home / Jiangxi Province / Wuyuan

City Overview

Wuyuan is known as 'China's most beautiful countryside.' This ancient county in northeastern Jiangxi is famous for its well-preserved Hui-style architecture, stunning rapeseed flower fields in spring, and vibrant autumn colors in stone villages that have existed for over 1,000 years.

📍 Location

Northeastern Jiangxi, bordering Anhui and Zhejiang. Part of the ancient Huizhou region.

🏘️ Hui-style Villages

Over 200 ancient villages with white walls, black tiles, and intricate wood/stone carvings.

🌼 Rapeseed Flowers

In spring, golden rapeseed fields surround ancient villages — China's most iconic rural landscape.

🍂 Autumn Colors

In November, maple trees and ginkgo trees turn villages into paintings of red, orange, and gold.

Top Attractions

🏘️ Huangling Village

A hillside village famous for 'shaiqiu' — drying crops in bamboo baskets on wooden frames. Spectacular in autumn.

🌼 Rapeseed Flower Fields

In March, the entire valley blooms golden — the best views are at Jiangwan and Likeng.

🏛️ Likeng Village

The most accessible ancient village — a peaceful stroll through 1,000 years of history.

🌳 Shicheng Village

Famous for ancient maple trees that turn brilliant red in November — a photographer's paradise.

Best Time to Visit Wuyuan

🌸 Spring (Mar-Apr)

PEAK SEASON. Rapeseed flowers in full bloom — the most famous sight. Book accommodation early.

☀️ Summer (Jun-Aug)

Hot and humid. Lush green landscapes. Fewer tourists. Good for village exploration.

🍂 Autumn (Oct-Nov)

SPECTACULAR. Maple and ginkgo trees turn red/gold. 'Shaiqiu' harvest displays in Huangling.

❄️ Winter (Dec-Feb)

Cold and quiet. Some snow on ancient rooftops. Very few tourists — authentic atmosphere.

Wuyuan Travel Tips

⚠️ Important Reminders

  • Best Time: March for rapeseed flowers OR November for autumn colors — these are THE seasons to visit.
  • Transport: Rent a car or hire a driver — villages are scattered and public transport is limited.
  • Huangling: Don't miss the 'shaiqiu' (晒秋) harvest display — most spectacular in October-November.
  • Accommodation: Stay in a village guesthouse for the full experience — especially magical at dawn.
