Why Visit China?

China offers an unparalleled travel experience that blends ancient traditions with modern innovation

🏯 Ancient History

5,000 years of civilization with 56 UNESCO World Heritage Sites. From the Great Wall to the Terracotta Warriors.

🍜 World-Class Cuisine

Eight major Chinese cuisines from spicy Sichuan to delicate Cantonese. Street food to fine dining experiences.

🏔️ Diverse Landscapes

From the Himalayas to tropical beaches, deserts to lush forests. Every region offers unique natural beauty.

🎭 Cultural Diversity

56 ethnic groups with unique traditions, languages, and festivals. Experience authentic cultural encounters.

🚄 Modern Infrastructure

High-speed trains, modern airports, and efficient public transit. Travel comfortably across vast distances.

💰 Great Value

High-quality experiences at affordable prices. Great accommodation, food, and transportation value.

China at a Glance

Essential information for planning your trip

📅 Best Time to Visit

April-May and September-October for most regions. Climate varies greatly from north to south.

💱 Currency

Chinese Yuan (CNY/RMB). Credit cards widely accepted in cities. Cash recommended for rural areas.

🗣️ Language

Mandarin is official. English spoken in tourist areas. Translation apps helpful for communication.

⚡ Electricity

220V/50Hz. Power outlets type A, I, with two or three prongs. Bring a universal adapter.

📱 Visa

Most visitors need a tourist visa (L visa). Some countries have visa-free transit options.

🕐 Time Zone

China Standard Time (CST): UTC+8. Single time zone across the entire country.

Essential Travel Tips

insider advice for a smooth and memorable journey

📱 Apps to Download

WeChat (essential for payment & communication), Alipay, Didi (ride-hailing), Pleco (translation), Ctrip (booking).

💳 Payment Tips

Link your card to WeChat/Alipay. Carry some cash. QR code payments are universal. Mobile payment is everywhere.

🚄 Getting Around

High-speed trains are fast and affordable. Didi is convenient for taxis. Metro systems in major cities are efficient.

🍽️ Dining Etiquette

Wait to be seated. Use chopsticks properly. Tapping chopsticks is rude. Leaving some food shows satisfaction.

📸 Photography

Ask permission before photographing people. Many attractions require separate photography tickets.

🛒 Shopping

Bargaining is expected at markets. Check quality carefully. Keep receipts for tax refunds at airports.
