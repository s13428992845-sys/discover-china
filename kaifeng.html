Kaifeng

Song Dynasty capital — night markets, the Iron Pagoda, Qingming Garden, and the best street food in Henan.

Home / Henan Province / Kaifeng

City Overview

Kaifeng was one of the world's largest cities during the Song Dynasty (960-1279). It's famous for its vibrant night markets, the Iron Pagoda, the recreated Qingming Shanghe Garden (a living Song Dynasty town), and the best street food in central China.

📍 Location

Eastern Henan, 80km east of Zhengzhou. Connected by highway and bus.

🏯 Song Capital

One of the world's largest cities in the 11th century — Marco Polo visited during its golden age.

🍜 Night Markets

China's most vibrant night food scene — the Gulou Night Market has hundreds of food stalls until dawn.

🏛️ Iron Pagoda

A 900-year-old iron-colored glazed pagoda — Kaifeng's most iconic landmark since 1049.

Top Attractions

🍜 Gulou Night Market

One of China's best night markets — hundreds of food stalls from 6PM to dawn. A foodie paradise.

🏛️ Qingming Garden

A living recreation of the famous Song Dynasty scroll — costumed actors, ancient buildings, and street performances.

🏛️ Iron Pagoda

900-year-old pagoda covered in glazed tiles that look like iron — climb for views over old Kaifeng.

🏛️ Dragon Pavilion

The former imperial palace site — a grand hall on a marble terrace overlooking Dragon Lake.

Best Time to Visit Kaifeng

🌸 Spring (Mar-May)

Pleasant warming. Chrysanthemum gardens preparing. Good for food exploration.

☀️ Summer (Jun-Aug)

Hot but the night markets come alive. Evening street food is the main activity.

🍂 Autumn (Sep-Nov)

Chrysanthemum Festival in October — the city blooms with thousands of flower varieties. BEST season.

❄️ Winter (Dec-Feb)

Cold. Night markets continue. Good for warming soups and street food.

Kaifeng Travel Tips

⚠️ Important Reminders

  • Night Market: Arrive hungry at Gulou Night Market by 7PM. Try tangbao (soup dumplings) and roasted sweet potatoes.
  • Chrysanthemum Festival: October-November. The city hosts elaborate flower exhibitions — it's Kaifeng's signature event.
  • Qingming Garden: Spend at least 3 hours — there are performances, food, and crafts throughout the day.
  • Song Dynasty Food: Try traditional Song Dynasty recipes still served in Kaifeng — it's 1,000 years of culinary tradition.
