Haikou

The capital of Hainan, a tropical city with historic Qilou Old Street, volcanic landscapes, and excellent seafood.

Home / Hainan Province / Haikou

City Overview

Haikou is the gateway to Hainan Island and its political center. Despite being a modern city, it preserves charming colonial architecture in the Qilou Old Street district. The city offers a relaxed tropical atmosphere, excellent street food, and serves as the perfect starting point for exploring the island.

📍 Location

Northern Hainan Island, facing Guangdong across the Qiongzhou Strait.

👥 Population

Approximately 2.9 million. Growing rapidly as a free-trade port city.

🌡️ Climate

Tropical, 22-30°C year-round. Wet season May-October.

🏛️ Character

Relaxed tropical city blending colonial heritage with modern development.

Top Attractions

🏛️ Qilou Old Street

Beautifully preserved colonial-era arcade street with shops, cafes, and local life spanning 100+ years.

🌋 Volcanic Cluster

Ancient volcanic crater parks with lush vegetation growing in craters. Unique geological experience.

🏖️ Holiday Beach

Popular urban beach with coconut groves, perfect for sunset walks and water activities.

🍜 Food Scene

Hainan's culinary capital — try Wenchang chicken, coconut rice, and fresh tropical fruits at night markets.

Best Time to Visit Haikou

🌸 Spring (Mar-May)

Warm and pleasant, 25-30°C. Occasional rain showers. Great for exploring.

☀️ Summer (Jun-Aug)

Hot and humid with typhoon risk. Good for indoor activities and beach time.

🍂 Autumn (Sep-Nov)

Cooling down, less rain. Ideal for sightseeing and outdoor activities.

❄️ Winter (Dec-Feb)

Warm and comfortable, 18-25°C. Peak season for 'snowbird' tourists from the north.

Haikou Travel Tips

⚠️ Important Reminders

  • Qilou at Night: The old street comes alive after dark with lanterns, street food, and local musicians.
  • Coconut Chicken: Try Hainan's signature dish — chicken cooked in fresh coconut water. Available citywide.
  • Ferry to Continent: If arriving by car, the ferry from Xuwen takes 1.5 hours. Book ahead in holidays.
  • Renting a Car: Recommended for exploring beyond Haikou. Roads are excellent.
