Yanji

The heart of Korean-Chinese culture — authentic Korean food, language, and traditions in China.

Home / Jilin Province / Yanji

City Overview

Yanji is the capital of the Yanbian Korean Autonomous Prefecture — a unique city where Korean language, food, and culture thrive alongside Chinese identity. Over 50% of the population is ethnic Korean. The city offers an authentic Korean experience without leaving China — from the signage to the cuisine to the language.

📍 Location

Eastern Jilin, near the borders with Russia and North Korea. Gateway to Changbai Mountain.

🇰🇷 Korean Culture

A uniquely Korean-Chinese city — over 50% ethnic Korean population. Bilingual signage everywhere.

🍜 Korean Food

The most authentic Korean food in China — cold noodles, barbecue, kimchi, and rice cake soup.

⛰️ Changbai Gateway

The main gateway to Changbai Mountain — 2 hours by road to the North Slope.

Top Attractions

🍜 Korean Food Streets

Experience authentic Korean-Chinese cuisine — cold noodles, bulgogi, bibimbap, and kimchi.

🏛️ Yanbian Museum

Learn about the unique Korean-Chinese culture — bilingual exhibitions, traditional costumes, and history.

🌳 People's Park

A peaceful park where locals practice traditional Korean activities — dancing, games, music.

🏔️ Changbai Mountain

The day-trip highlight — Tianchi (Heaven Lake) is 2 hours away. Base yourself in Yanji.

Best Time to Visit Yanji

🌸 Spring (Mar-May)

Cool spring. Cherry blossoms in April-May. Korean-Chinese festivals begin.

☀️ Summer (Jun-Aug)

Warm (20-26°C). Best time for Changbai Mountain. Lush forests and wildflowers.

🍂 Autumn (Sep-Oct)

Beautiful fall colors. Changbai Mountain at its most spectacular. Harvest festivals.

❄️ Winter (Nov-Mar)

Very cold (-20 to -10°C). Korean hot food is especially warming. Indoor cultural experiences.

Yanji Travel Tips

⚠️ Important Reminders

  • Korean Food: Try 冷面 (cold noodles) — Yanji's signature dish. The most authentic outside of Korea.
  • Changbai Mountain: Base yourself in Yanji and take a day trip — hire a driver for the 2-hour journey.
  • Language: Korean is widely spoken — many signs are bilingual. A unique cultural experience within China.
  • Border Area: Yanji is close to the North Korean border — you can see across the Tumen River from nearby towns.
