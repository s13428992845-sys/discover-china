Yangshuo

A backpacker paradise of karst peaks, rice paddies, and a lively riverside town.

Home / Guangxi / Yangshuo

City Overview

Yangshuo is a small town that has become one of China's most beloved destinations for both domestic and international travelers. Nestled among the most dramatic karst scenery in the world, it offers cycling through rice paddies, bamboo rafting on the Yulong River, rock climbing, and a vibrant nightlife scene along West Street.

📍 Location

65km south of Guilin. Connected by bus (1.5 hours) or Li River cruise (4 hours).

🚲 Cycling Paradise

Cycling through rice paddies and karst peaks — one of China's most rewarding rural experiences.

🎋 Yulong River

Peaceful bamboo raft rides through the most picturesque countryside in China.

🧗 Rock Climbing

World-class limestone climbing — Moon Hill and nearby crags attract climbers globally.

Top Attractions

🚲 Countryside Cycling

Rent a bike and explore the flat rice paddies surrounded by towering karst peaks.

🌙 Moon Hill

Climb to the natural arch at the top — stunning views of the karst landscape at sunset.

🎋 Yulong River Raft

A peaceful 2-hour bamboo raft ride — far more scenic than the Li River cruise.

🏮 West Street

The famous backpacker bar street — lively nightlife, street food, and international atmosphere.

Best Time to Visit Yangshuo

🌸 Spring (Mar-May)

Beautiful. Misty mornings over rice paddies. Wildflowers. Pleasant cycling weather.

☀️ Summer (Jun-Aug)

Hot. River activities are refreshing. Rice paddies lush green. Rain possible.

🍂 Autumn (Sep-Nov)

BEST SEASON. Golden rice harvest, clear skies, perfect cycling weather.

❄️ Winter (Dec-Feb)

Cool and quiet. Few tourists. Atmospheric misty mornings over the karst peaks.

Yangshuo Travel Tips

⚠️ Important Reminders

  • Cycling: Rent a bike or electric scooter — the countryside routes between Yangshuo and Gaotian are the most scenic.
  • Yulong River Raft: The raft from Jinlong Bridge to Gongnong Bridge is the best section — about 2 hours.
  • Moon Hill: Climb at sunset for the best views. The donkey ride up is part of the fun.
  • Impression Sanjie Liu: An outdoor night show directed by Zhang Yimou — performed on the Li River with the karst peaks as backdrop. Book in advance.
