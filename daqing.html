Daqing

China's oil capital — vast wetlands, hot springs, and a city built on black gold.

Home / Heilongjiang Province / Daqing

City Overview

Daqing is one of China's most important oil cities — the 'Daqing Oil Field' was a symbol of Chinese industrial achievement. Beyond the oil derricks that dot the landscape, the city offers surprising attractions: vast wetlands teeming with birds, natural hot springs, and a unique industrial heritage museum.

📍 Location

Western Heilongjiang, 150km west of Harbin. HSR connection available.

🛢️ Oil Capital

China's largest oil field since the 1960s — a symbol of China's industrial revolution.

🌿 Wetland Paradise

Extensive wetlands around the city — important bird habitats and natural beauty.

♨️ Hot Springs

Natural hot springs in the surrounding area — perfect for relaxation after industrial sightseeing.

Top Attractions

🛢️ Oil Field Museum

Learn about China's oil industry — the Daqing Spirit is a key part of modern Chinese history.

🌿 Zhalong Nature Reserve

A world-famous wetland — home to red-crowned cranes and hundreds of bird species.

♨️ Hot Springs

Several hot spring resorts near Daqing — natural thermal waters for relaxation.

🏛️ Iron Man Museum

Dedicated to Wang Jinxi, China's model worker — a fascinating window into Chinese industrial culture.

Best Time to Visit Daqing

🌸 Spring (Mar-May)

Warming up. Migratory birds return to wetlands. May is pleasant.

☀️ Summer (Jun-Aug)

Warm (22-28°C). Wetlands at their most beautiful. Bird watching season.

🍂 Autumn (Sep-Oct)

Migratory birds depart. Comfortable temperatures. Beautiful wetland colors.

❄️ Winter (Nov-Mar)

Very cold (-20 to -10°C). Hot springs are most enjoyable in winter. Indoor museums.

Daqing Travel Tips

⚠️ Important Reminders

  • Zhalong Reserve: Visit in April-May or September-October for the best bird watching — red-crowned cranes are the highlight.
  • Hot Springs: The best way to experience Daqing in winter — soak in natural hot springs while it snows.
  • Oil Heritage: The Daqing Oil Field is a UNESCO-recognized industrial heritage site — the museum tells a fascinating story.
  • Day Trip: Zhalong is 30km south of Daqing — combine with a hot spring visit for a full day trip.
