Luoyang

Ancient capital — the magnificent Longmen Grottoes, White Horse Temple (China's first Buddhist temple), and peony flowers.

Home / Henan Province / Luoyang

City Overview

Luoyang served as capital for 13 Chinese dynasties over 4,000 years. It's famous for the Longmen Grottoes — 100,000 Buddhist statues carved into limestone cliffs — and as the birthplace of Chinese Buddhism at the White Horse Temple.

📍 Location

Western Henan, on the Luo River. 150km west of Zhengzhou by high-speed train (45 min).

🗿 Grottoes

Longmen Grottoes — 100,000 Buddhist statues in 2,345 caves spanning 400 years of carving. UNESCO World Heritage.

🏛️ First Temple

White Horse Temple (68 AD) — China's first Buddhist temple, founded when Buddhism arrived via the Silk Road.

🌸 Peonies

Luoyang is China's peony capital — the annual April Peony Festival transforms the city with millions of flowers.

Top Attractions

🗿 Longmen Grottoes

UNESCO masterpiece — the Vairocana Buddha (Fengxian Temple) is China's finest stone sculpture.

🏛️ White Horse Temple

China's first Buddhist temple (68 AD) — 2,000 years of continuous worship. Serene and atmospheric.

🌸 Peony Gardens

In April, the city explodes with peonies — China's 'King of Flowers.' Visit the National Peony Garden.

🏛️ Luoyang Museum

Excellent collection of Tang Dynasty tri-color pottery and ancient bronze vessels from imperial tombs.

Best Time to Visit Luoyang

🌸 Spring (Mar-May)

BEST season — April Peony Festival is spectacular. Warm, colorful, and atmospheric.

☀️ Summer (Jun-Aug)

Hot (35°C+). Visit grottoes early morning. Evenings are pleasant along the Yi River.

🍂 Autumn (Sep-Nov)

Cool and comfortable. Fewer crowds at the grottoes. Beautiful fall light for photography.

❄️ Winter (Dec-Feb)

Cold (-5 to 5°C). Very few tourists. Grottoes are atmospheric in winter quiet.

Luoyang Travel Tips

⚠️ Important Reminders

  • Peony Festival: Mid-April is peak bloom. Book accommodation 2 months ahead — it's extremely popular.
  • Longmen Timing: Afternoon light illuminates the west cliff (Fengxian Temple) best. Allow 3-4 hours.
  • White Horse: Visit early morning when monks chant. The atmosphere is completely different from tourist hours.
  • Water Banquet: Try the famous Luoyang Water Banquet (shuixi) — 24 courses of soups and broths.
