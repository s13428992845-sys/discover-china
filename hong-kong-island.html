Hong Kong Island

The glittering heart of Hong Kong — Victoria Peak, Central district, and iconic harbour views.

Home / Hong Kong / Hong Kong Island

City Overview

Hong Kong Island is the urban core of the city — where gleaming skyscrapers rise above bustling street markets, where Michelin-starred restaurants sit next to century-old temples. From the Peak's panoramic views to the lively streets of Central and Causeway Bay, this is Hong Kong at its most iconic.

📍 Location

The northern part of Hong Kong. Connected to Kowloon by harbour crossings.

🌃 Victoria Peak

The island's crown — 552m of panoramic views over the harbour and city skyline.

🏙️ Central District

The financial heart — a mix of skyscrapers, art galleries, and hidden food gems.

🚡 Peak Tram

The historic funicular since 1888 — the classic way to reach Victoria Peak.

Top Attractions

🌃 Victoria Peak

Take the Peak Tram and enjoy the world-famous panoramic view — best at sunset or night.

🏮 Temple Street

The most famous night market — street food, fortune tellers, and lively bargaining.

🏖️ Repulse Bay

Hong Kong's most beautiful beach — a crescent of sand with a relaxed atmosphere.

🏛️ Man Mo Temple

A 150-year-old temple on Hollywood Road — incense coils spiral from the ceiling.

Best Time to Visit Hong Kong Island

🌸 Spring (Mar-May)

Warm and humid. Some fog. Comfortable for walking and outdoor activities.

☀️ Summer (Jun-Aug)

Hot and humid. Typhoon season. Best for beaches and indoor attractions.

🍂 Autumn (Sep-Nov)

Best season. Clear skies, pleasant temperatures. Perfect for Peak views and hiking.

❄️ Winter (Dec-Feb)

Cool and dry. Comfortable for walking. Great for shopping and food tours.

Hong Kong Island Travel Tips

⚠️ Important Reminders

  • Peak Tram: Go right at opening (8am) or late evening to avoid long queues. The view is worth any wait.
  • Hiking: The Dragon's Back trail (Shek O) is rated one of the world's best urban hikes — stunning coastal views.
  • Dim Sum: Tim Ho Wan in Central is the world's cheapest Michelin-starred restaurant — expect a queue.
  • Octopus Card: Essential for all transport. Buy one at any MTR station upon arrival.
