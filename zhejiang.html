West Lake's timeless beauty, ancient water towns, and the prosperity of Hangzhou — Marco Polo's 'City of Heaven'.

Province Overview

West Lake's timeless beauty, ancient water towns, and the prosperity of Hangzhou — Marco Polo's 'City of Heaven'.

📍 Location

Eastern China, on the East China Sea coast. South of Shanghai. Borders Jiangsu, Anhui, Jiangxi, and Fujian.

👥 Population

Approximately 65 million. One of China's wealthiest provinces, birthplace of Alibaba and the private economy.

🌡️ Climate

Subtropical monsoon. Hot humid summers, cool winters. Best visited Mar-May and Sep-Nov.

🏔️ Terrain

70% mountains and hills, 30% plains. Famous for West Lake, Qiantang River, and thousands of islands.

🍜 Cuisine

Zhejiang cuisine — one of China's Eight Great Cuisines. Fresh, light flavors. Dongpo pork, West Lake fish, Longjing tea.

✈️ Major Airport

Hangzhou Xiaoshan (HGH) — major hub with extensive domestic and international connections.

Why Visit Zhejiang Province?

🏞️ West Lake

UNESCO World Heritage — the most famous lake in China, inspiring poets and painters for 1,000+ years. Willow-lined shores and pagodas.

🏘️ Wuzhen

The quintessential Jiangnan water town — ancient canals, stone bridges, and traditional workshops. Best preserved in China.

🍵 Longjing Tea

Visit the legendary Dragon Well tea villages — pick tea in spring and taste the freshest green tea in China.

🌊 Qiantang Tides

The world's largest tidal bore — a spectacular natural phenomenon reaching 9 meters high every autumn.

🏔️ Putuo Mountain

One of China's Four Sacred Buddhist Mountains — island monastery with ocean views and deep spirituality.

🏯 Lingyin Temple

One of China's largest and wealthiest Buddhist temples — 1,700 years old, nestled in scenic forested hills.

Best Time to Visit

🌸 Spring (Mar-May)

Perfect season. Peach blossoms around West Lake, tea picking season, pleasant temperatures throughout.

☀️ Summer (Jun-Aug)

Hot and humid (35°C+). Plum rains in June. Lotus flowers on West Lake are beautiful. Escape to mountain resorts.

🍂 Autumn (Sep-Nov)

Ideal weather. Clear skies, comfortable temps. Full moon over West Lake is legendary in autumn.

❄️ Winter (Dec-Feb)

Cold (0-8°C) but West Lake's snowy scenery is poetic. Fewer crowds, lower prices.

Getting Around Zhejiang Province

✈️ By Air

  • Main Airport: Hangzhou Xiaoshan (HGH) — one of China's busiest airports. Flights to most domestic and international destinations.
  • Other: Ningbo Lishe (NGB), Wenzhou, Zhoushan airports also available.

🚄 By Train

  • High-Speed Rail: Hangzhou to Shanghai (1h), Nanjing (1.5h), Beijing (5h). Extremely well-connected.
  • Coverage: Every major city in Zhejiang has HSR stations.

🚇 By Metro

  • Hangzhou Metro: 12+ lines covering West Lake, city center, and suburbs. Modern and efficient.
  • Other: Ningbo, Wenzhou, and Shaoxing also have metro systems.

🚕 By Taxi

  • Availability: Taxis and Didi widely available. Hangzhou is very tech-friendly.
  • Pricing: Starting at 11-13 CNY. Cashless payment standard.

Zhejiang Province Travel Tips

⚠️ Important Reminders

  • West Lake Circuit: Rent a bike to circle West Lake — about 15km. Stop at Leifeng Pagoda for panoramic views.
  • Tea Experience: Visit Longjing Village in March-April for tea picking. Buy directly from farmers for authentic tea.
  • Wuzhen Overnight: Stay inside Wuzhen's scenic area to experience the water town after day-trippers leave.
  • Lingyin Early: Arrive at Lingyin Temple before 7AM to experience morning chanting and avoid crowds.
  • Food Guide: Try Dongpo pork, West Lake vinegar fish, and Longjing shrimp at restaurants near West Lake.
  • Qiantang Tide: Best viewed on the 18th day of the 8th lunar month (usually September/October).
  • Putuo Island: Allow 2 days. Stay overnight to experience the island's spiritual atmosphere at dawn.
  • Payment: Cash rarely used. Set up Alipay or WeChat Pay. Hangzhou is China's cashless capital.
