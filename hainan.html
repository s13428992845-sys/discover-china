Hainan Province

Known as "China's Hawaii" — pristine tropical beaches, coconut palms, crystal-clear waters, and year-round sunshine on China's southernmost island.

Home / Provinces / Hainan Province

Province Overview

Hainan is China's southernmost tropical island province, often called 'China's Hawaii'. With beautiful beaches, lush tropical rainforests, and a growing international tourism industry, it offers a perfect blend of relaxation and adventure. The free-trade port policy makes it increasingly attractive to international visitors.

📍 Location

Southernmost China, a tropical island in the South China Sea. Separated from Guangdong by the Qiongzhou Strait.

👥 Population

Approximately 10 million. A melting pot of Han, Li, Miao, and other ethnic groups.

🌡️ Climate

Tropical monsoon climate, 24-28°C year-round. Dry season Nov-Feb, wet season May-Oct. Typhoon season Aug-Oct.

🏔️ Terrain

Tropical island with central mountain range (Wuzhi Shan 1,867m), coastal plains, rainforests, and coral reefs.

🍜 Cuisine

Fresh seafood, Wenchang chicken, Hele crab, Dongshan mutton, tropical fruits, and coconut-based dishes.

✈️ Major Airport

Haikou Meilan (HAK) and Sanya Phoenix (SYX) — both international airports with growing route networks.

Why Visit Hainan Province?

🏖️ Tropical Beaches

Yalong Bay, Dadonghai, and Wuzhizhou Island offer world-class beaches with clear turquoise waters.

🌴 Wuzhizhou Island

China's premier diving and snorkeling destination with coral reefs and marine life.

🏔️ Wuzhi Mountain

The 'Roof of Hainan' — sacred mountain with stunning sunrise views and lush rainforest trails.

🌺 Tropical Rainforest

Yanoda and Jianfengling reserves feature rare tropical flora, canopy walks, and waterfalls.

🍤 Fresh Seafood

Hainan's seafood markets serve the freshest catch — lobsters, groupers, and tropical fish at great prices.

🛍️ Duty-Free Shopping

Hainan is China's duty-free paradise — luxury shopping with annual allowances up to 100,000 CNY.

Best Time to Visit

🌸 Spring (March - May)

Warm and pleasant, ideal for beach activities. Water temperatures rise. Perfect for surfing and diving.

☀️ Summer (June - August)

Hot and humid with occasional typhoons. Great for water sports but bring sun protection.

🍂 Autumn (September - November)

Comfortable temperatures, less rain. Peak tourist season begins. Best overall time to visit.

❄️ Winter (December - February)

Warm escape from northern winters. 20-25°C. Peak season — book accommodations early.

Getting Around Hainan Province

✈️ By Air

  • Major Airports: Haikou Meilan (HAK) and Sanya Phoenix (SYX) with flights across China and Asia
  • Ring Expressway: Hainan has a high-speed rail loop connecting all major coastal cities
  • Budget Tips: Off-season flights (May-Jun, Sep-Oct) can be very cheap

🚄 By Train

  • High-Speed Rail: Hainan ring HSR connects Haikou to Sanya in 1.5 hours
  • Cross-Sea Ferry: Train ferries across Qiongzhou Strait from Guangdong
  • Scenic Route: The eastern coastal line offers beautiful ocean views

🚌 By Bus

  • Intercity Buses: Connect all towns and tourist areas across the island
  • Tourist Shuttles: Direct buses from airports to major resorts
  • Local Transit: City buses and taxis available in all towns

🚗 Car Rental

  • Self-Drive: Excellent expressways make road trips easy and scenic
  • Ring Road: The coastal expressway circles the entire island
  • EV Friendly: Growing charging station network across the island

Hainan Province Travel Tips

⚠️ Important Reminders

  • Duty-Free Shopping: Non-Hainan residents can buy up to 100,000 CNY duty-free per year. Bring your ID and flight info.
  • Sun Protection: Tropical UV is intense. Bring SPF50+ sunscreen, hat, and sunglasses even in winter.
  • Typhoon Season: Aug-Oct brings typhoons. Check weather forecasts and have backup plans.
  • Beach Safety: Only swim at designated beaches with lifeguards. Some areas have dangerous currents.
  • Tropical Fruits: Try local fruits at markets — mangoes, lychees, longans, and coconuts are incredibly fresh and cheap.
  • Li Ethnic Culture: Visit Li ethnic villages to see traditional brocade weaving and bamboo dance performances.
  • Visa-Free: Hainan offers 30-day visa-free entry for tourists from 59 countries.
  • Seafood Tips: Buy fresh seafood at markets and have restaurants cook it for you — cheaper and fresher.
