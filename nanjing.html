Nanjing

Ancient capital of six dynasties — Ming City Wall, Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum, and vibrant university town.

Home / Jiangsu Province / Nanjing

City Overview

Nanjing, once capital of six Chinese dynasties, blends deep history with modern vitality. The massive Ming City Wall encircles the city center, while Purple Mountain hosts imperial tombs and ancient temples. Today it's a thriving educational and cultural hub.

📍 Location

Western Jiangsu, on the Yangtze River. 300km from Shanghai via HSR.

🏛️ History

Capital of six dynasties, including the Ming Dynasty (initially). 2,500 years of history.

🎓 Education

One of China's top university cities with over 80 universities and research institutes.

🌡️ Climate

Four distinct seasons. Hot summers, cool winters. Spring and autumn are ideal.

Top Attractions

🏯 Ming City Wall

The world's longest city wall (33.7km). Walk sections near Zhonghua Gate for the best experience.

🏛️ Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum

The founding father's grand tomb on Purple Mountain — 392 steps to the memorial hall.

🏘️ Confucius Temple Area

The Fuzimiao area — bustling streets, Qinhuai River boat rides, and traditional architecture.

🕯️ Massacre Memorial

The Nanjing Massacre Memorial Hall — a moving and essential testament to history. Free entry.

Best Time to Visit Nanjing

🌸 Spring (Mar-May)

Cherry blossoms at Jiming Temple. Pleasant weather for walking the City Wall.

☀️ Summer (Jun-Aug)

Very hot (35°C+). Visit museums and indoor sites. Plum rain season in June.

🍂 Autumn (Sep-Nov)

Best season. Cool, clear. Purple Mountain's autumn foliage is stunning.

❄️ Winter (Dec-Feb)

Cold (0-5°C). Few tourists. Good for museum visits and hot food tours.

Nanjing Travel Tips

⚠️ Important Reminders

  • Must-Eat: Nanjing salted duck (yanshui ya) — the city's signature dish. Try it at Hanfuqiao food street.
  • City Wall Walk: Rent a bike and ride along the top of the Ming City Wall — unique perspective of the city.
  • Purple Mountain: Allow a full day for the mausoleum, Linggu Temple, and the天文台 (observatory).
  • Qinhuai Night: Take a boat ride on the Qinhuai River at night — the illuminated ancient buildings are magical.
