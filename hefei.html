Hefei

The provincial capital — modern tech hub with historical sites like Bao Zheng Temple and Chaohu Lake.

Home / Anhui Province / Hefei

City Overview

Hefei is a rapidly developing tech hub that still preserves its historical heritage. Home to Bao Zheng (China's most famous judge), the city offers a mix of modern innovation and ancient culture, with Chaohu Lake providing natural beauty.

📍 Location

Central Anhui, between the Yangtze and Huai rivers. A major high-speed rail hub.

🏛️ History

Birthplace of Bao Zheng, China's most revered judge. 2,000+ years of history.

🔬 Science City

Home to the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) — a leading research hub.

🌊 Chaohu Lake

One of China's five largest freshwater lakes, now part of Hefei city.

Top Attractions

🏛️ Bao Zheng Temple

Dedicated to China's most famous judge — a symbol of justice and integrity. Beautiful gardens.

🌊 Chaohu Lake

Boat rides, fishing villages, and lakeside cycling. Less touristy and very authentic.

🏘️ Sanhe Ancient Town

A 2,500-year-old water town south of Hefei — traditional architecture, local snacks, and peaceful canals.

🏛️ Anhui Museum

Excellent collection of Hui culture artifacts, ancient bronzes, and Song Dynasty paintings.

Best Time to Visit Hefei

🌸 Spring (Mar-May)

Pleasant and warm. Peach blossoms around Chaohu Lake. Ideal for outdoor activities.

☀️ Summer (Jun-Aug)

Hot and humid (35°C+). Best for indoor museum visits and evening lake walks.

🍂 Autumn (Sep-Nov)

Best season. Comfortable temperatures, clear skies. Great for day trips.

❄️ Winter (Dec-Feb)

Cold (0-5°C). Few tourists. Good for hot food and indoor attractions.

Hefei Travel Tips

⚠️ Important Reminders

  • Food: Try Hefei's signature dishes — 曹操鸡 (Cao Cao's chicken) and 吴山贡鹅 (Wushan goose).
  • Sanhe Town: A peaceful half-day trip from Hefei. Try the local rice wine and fried tofu.
  • Bao Zheng: The temple complex is a symbol of Chinese justice — interesting cultural insight.
  • Transit Hub: Hefei's HSR station connects to Shanghai (3h), Nanjing (1.5h), and Wuhan (2h).
