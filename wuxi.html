Wuxi

Taihu Lake city famous for sweetness, Taihu stones, and the stunning Yuantouzhu peninsula.

Home / Jiangsu Province / Wuxi

City Overview

Wuxi sits on the shores of Lake Tai (Taihu), China's third-largest freshwater lake. Known for its sweet cuisine, delicate silk, and the famous Yuantouzhu (Turtle Head Isle) — one of China's best cherry blossom spots.

📍 Location

Southern Jiangsu, on the northern shore of Taihu Lake. Between Nanjing and Shanghai.

🌸 Cherry Blossoms

Yuantouzhu is one of China's top 3 cherry blossom spots. Spectacular in late March.

🍜 Sweet Cuisine

Wuxi cuisine is famous for its sweetness — Wuxi ribs, sweet mandarin fish, and xiaolongbao.

🏛️ Grand Canal

The ancient Grand Canal runs through Wuxi — boat rides offer a unique water-town perspective.

Top Attractions

🌸 Yuantouzhu

Turtle Head Isle on Taihu Lake — cherry blossoms in spring, lotus in summer, and stunning sunsets year-round.

🏛️ Lingshan Grand Buddha

An 88-meter-tall bronze Buddha — one of the tallest in the world. Impressive Buddhist complex.

🏘️ Huishan Ancient Town

A well-preserved historic district with ancestral halls, clay figurines, and local snacks.

🎭 Kunqu Opera

Wuxi is a center for Kunqu — China's oldest opera form. Catch a performance if possible.

Best Time to Visit Wuxi

🌸 Spring (Mar-May)

Best season. Cherry blossoms at Yuantouzhu (late March) are spectacular.

☀️ Summer (Jun-Aug)

Hot and humid. Taihu Lake breezes provide some relief. Lotus flowers bloom.

🍂 Autumn (Sep-Nov)

Comfortable weather. Taihu fish are at their best. Clear lake views.

❄️ Winter (Dec-Feb)

Cold but mild. Fewer tourists. Hot springs around Taihu are a nice treat.

Wuxi Travel Tips

⚠️ Important Reminders

  • Cherry Blossoms: Visit Yuantouzhu in late March — book tickets online in advance. Weekday mornings are less crowded.
  • Taihu Food: Try Taihu lake fish — especially the 'Three Whites' (white fish, white shrimp, silver fish).
  • Lingshan: The Grand Buddha complex is huge — allow 3 hours. The梵宫 (Brahma Palace) interior is stunning.
  • Day Trip: Wuxi is easily combined with Suzhou or Nanjing as a day trip via HSR.
