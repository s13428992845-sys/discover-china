Jiuzhaigou Valley

A UNESCO World Heritage fairyland of turquoise lakes, multi-tiered waterfalls, and snow-capped peaks in northern Sichuan.

Home / Sichuan / Jiuzhaigou

📍 Top Attractions

Five Flower Lake (五花海)

The crown jewel of Jiuzhaigou — crystal-clear turquoise waters revealing ancient fallen trees below.

Nuorilang Waterfall (诺日朗瀑布)

One of China's widest waterfalls, cascading 24.5 meters across a 270-meter-wide travertine ledge.

Mirror Lake & Pearl Shoal

Perfectly still waters that mirror the surrounding peaks, and a wide, shallow cascade of sparkling water.

🎫 Tickets & Hours

  • Peak Season (Apr 1–Nov 15): ¥169 + ¥90 shuttle bus
  • Off Season (Nov 16–Mar 31): ¥80 + ¥80 shuttle bus
  • Opening Hours: 8:00 AM – 5:00 PM
  • Book online in advance — daily visitor limit of 41,000

🚄 Getting There

✈️ By Air

  • Jiuzhai Huanglong Airport (JZH)
  • Flights from Chengdu (~55 min)
  • Airport shuttle to park (~2 hrs)

🚄 By High-Speed Rail

  • Chengdu to Huanglongjiuzhai Station
  • ~2 hours by high-speed train
  • Bus transfer to the park entrance

🚌 By Bus

  • Chengdu Chadianzi Bus Station
  • ~8-10 hours (scenic route)
  • Overnight sleeper buses available

💡 Travel Tips

  • Best visit: October for autumn colors; May–June for lush greenery
  • Arrive at park opening (8 AM) to beat the crowds
  • Altitude ranges from 2,000–4,500m — prepare for altitude sickness
  • Shuttle buses run throughout the park — plan your route in advance
