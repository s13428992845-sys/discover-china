Zhangye

Home to the spectacular Rainbow Mountains and the largest outdoor reclining Buddha in China.

Home / Gansu Province / Zhangye

City Overview

Zhangye is a Silk Road oasis city famous for the Danxia Landform — surreal rainbow-colored rock formations that look like a painter's palette. The city also has significant Buddhist heritage and serves as a gateway to the Qilian Mountains.

📍 Location

Central Hexi Corridor, Gansu. Key stop between Lanzhou and Dunhuang on the ancient Silk Road.

🌈 Danxia

The Rainbow Mountains — UNESCO Global Geopark with vivid red, yellow, orange, and green striped rock formations.

🏛️ Buddha Temple

Home to China's largest outdoor reclining Buddha — a 35m-long sleeping Buddha from the Western Xia Dynasty.

🏔️ Qilian Mountains

Snow-capped peaks to the south with Tibetan yak herders and alpine grasslands.

Top Attractions

🌈 Danxia Scenic Area

Four viewing platforms with different perspectives. Platform 1 and 4 offer the best sunset colors.

🏛️ Giant Buddha Temple

Built in 1098, houses a 34.5m reclining Buddha — the largest in China. Beautiful painted interior.

🏔️ Binggou Danxia

The lesser-known northern section — frozen-looking rock formations especially beautiful in winter.

🌾 Pingdan Lake

A man-made lake that perfectly reflects the colorful Danxia mountains — stunning at sunrise.

Best Time to Visit Zhangye

🌸 Spring (Mar-May)

Warming up. Clear skies and good visibility for photography. Fewer tourists.

☀️ Summer (Jun-Aug)

Warm and pleasant. Peak season for photography. Sunset light shows at Danxia are spectacular.

🍂 Autumn (Sep-Nov)

Best season. Golden autumn grasses contrast with the colorful rocks. Cool and dry.

❄️ Winter (Dec-Feb)

Very cold but the 'Ice Danxia' at Binggou is uniquely beautiful with snow-covered formations.

Zhangye Travel Tips

⚠️ Important Reminders

  • Sunset Timing: Arrive at Danxia by 4PM for the evening color show. Colors intensify dramatically as the sun lowers.
  • Platform Strategy: Start at Platform 4 (best sunset), then Platform 1, then Platforms 2 and 3.
  • Photography: Bring a polarizing filter for the best rainbow effect. No tripods allowed inside.
  • Combine with Jiayuguan: Zhangye and Jiayuguan can be done in 2 days — both are on the Hexi Corridor route.
