Lijiang

Where the ancient Naxi culture meets dramatic Himalayan scenery. From UNESCO-listed old town to the world's deepest gorge, Lijiang offers unforgettable experiences.

Home / Yunnan / Lijiang

City Overview

📍 Location

Northwestern Yunnan, 520km from Kunming. Gateway to the Hengduan Mountains. Ancient capital of the Naxi Kingdom. Elevation 2,400m — mild climate but thin air.

👥 Population

~1.3 million in Lijiang. Old Town (古城区) has ~30,000 residents. Large tourism industry but still retains authentic Naxi culture.

🏛️ Character

UNESCO World Heritage. Naxi minority culture, unique Dongba script, ancient music (Naxi Ancient Music). Cobblestone canals, wooden bridges, minority villages nearby.

🌡️ Climate

Warm days, cool nights. Best: March-June for flowers, September-November for weather. Strong sun, bring sunscreen. Rainy July-August.

💡 Why Visit Lijiang?

  • UNESCO Heritage: Perfectly preserved old town with 800+ years of history
  • Naxi Culture: Living culture — music, traditions, Dongba religion
  • Mountain Views: Jade Dragon Snow Mountain as backdrop, visible everywhere
  • Adventure Hub: Tiger Leaping Gorge trekking, hiking, climbing
  • Minority Villages: Baisha, Shuhe, Lugu Lake Mosuo villages nearby

Top Attractions

Lijiang Old Town - UNESCO

Lijiang Old Town (大研古城)

UNESCO World Heritage site with 800+ years of history. Unlike other "old towns" in China, this is a living community — locals still draw water from canals, dry clothes in courtyards, and live traditional lives. Best at dawn before shops open, or after 10 PM when tour groups leave. Climb Lion Hill (狮子山) for the classic postcard view of the town with Jade Dragon Snow Mountain.

💰 Ticket: ¥50 (visiting card, some areas)
🕐 Best Time: Early morning or late evening
📍 Location: City Center
UNESCO Culture Photography
Jade Dragon Snow Mountain

Jade Dragon Snow Mountain (玉龙雪山)

Sacred mountain of the Naxi people, standing at 5,596m. The 12 peaks are often hidden in clouds but appear dramatically when clear. Take the cable car to 4,506m. Below the mountain, Blue Valley (牦牛坪) offers hiking. The mountain is the backdrop for "Impression Lijiang" — a large-scale outdoor cultural show directed by Zhang Yimou.

💰 Ticket: ¥100 (park) + ¥140-180 (cable car)
🕐 Open: 6:30 AM - 5:00 PM
⚠️ Altitude: 3,000-4,500m
Nature Hiking Must-See
Tiger Leaping Gorge

Tiger Leaping Gorge (虎跳峡)

One of the world's deepest river canyons (3,793m), carved by the Yangtze River. Dramatic hiking trail along cliff edges with river roaring below. The upper trail (28 bends) offers the most spectacular views. For experienced hikers only in rainy season. One of National Geographic's "World's Best Hikes."

💰 Ticket: ¥45-65 (upper/lower gorge)
🕐 Best Season: May-November
📍 Distance: ~100km, 2-3 hours
Adventure Hiking Nature
Mu Palace

Mu Palace (木府)

"Little Forbidden City" — former residence of the Naxi chieftains who ruled Lijiang for over 400 years. Built in the Ming Dynasty with contributions from the imperial court. Beautiful traditional architecture, gardens, and exhibits on Naxi history. Audio guide included — worth the small extra cost.

💰 Ticket: ¥60 (includes audio guide)
🕐 Open: 8:30 AM - 6:00 PM
📍 Location: Old Town Center
History Architecture Culture
Dongba Museum

Dongba Culture & Naxi Ancient Music

Experience the living Dongba religion — unique to the Naxi, one of the few remaining hieroglyphic writing systems in the world. Attend an evening performance of Naxi Ancient Music (纳西古乐), played on traditional instruments and recognized as "living fossil music." Tickets available at the Dongba Cultural Center.

💰 Ticket: ¥100-280 (show varies)
🕐 Show Time: 8:00 PM nightly
📍 Location: Old Town
Culture Music Heritage

Local Food & Restaurants

Naxi cuisine with influences from Tibet, Sichuan, and Yunnan

🍲

Pork Trotter with Chili (Huang Man Luo)

Slow-braised pork knuckle, crispy skin, tender meat, served cold with spicy chili sauce. Acquired taste, but locals swear by it. Found at small eateries in old town and local restaurants.

🥘

Naxi Hotpot (Naxi Huo Guo)

Local specialty hot pot with yak meat, mushrooms, and fresh vegetables. Different from Sichuan hot pot — milder broth, unique dipping sauces. "Old Town Hotpot" and "Remote Rural" are popular spots.

🥐

Yak Meat (Zang Rou)

Lean, protein-rich meat from Tibetan-influenced cuisine. Yak jerky, yak sausage, stir-fried yak. Higher altitude villages like Shuhe serve the freshest yak dishes.

🍵

Pu'er Tea & Yak Butter Tea

Lijiang's high altitude makes it perfect for Pu'er aging. Try traditional yak butter tea — salty, warming, an acquired taste but authentic. Available at tea houses near Mu Palace.

🥔

Jelly Noodles (Liang Fen)

Cold rice noodle dish with spicy sauce, peanuts, and vegetables. Refreshing and cheap (¥5-10). Perfect for hot summer days. Found at street stalls near Four Streets.

🍽️ Where to Eat

  • Old Town Restaurants: Mid-range to upscale Yunnan/Naxi cuisine, tourist prices
  • Four Streets (新华街): Traditional Naxi small dishes, local atmosphere
  • Guan Creek Area: Cafes and Western food for homesick travelers
  • Shuhe Old Town: Quieter, more authentic, cheaper than main old town

Transportation Guide

✈️ Getting to Lijiang

  • Train from Kunming: ¥150-200, 3.5-4 hours. New high-speed line opened.
  • Flight: Lijiang Sanyuan Airport (LJG). Domestic flights from major cities.
  • Bus from Dali: ~3 hours, scenic mountain road. ¥60-80.
  • Shuttle from Tiger Leaping Gorge: Regular buses from Lijiang.

🚌 Getting Around

  • Walking: Old Town is pedestrian-only. Navigate by landmarks (木府, 四方街).
  • Didi: Reliable, ¥10-30 within city. Drop-off points outside old town.
  • Bus: Multiple lines to attractions. ¥2-5. Less convenient for Old Town.
  • Bicycle: Limited, uneven cobblestones. Electric bikes better.

🚐 Day Trips & Tours

  • Jade Dragon Snow Mountain: Organized tour ¥400-600 or bus to Genting ski area
  • Tiger Leaping Gorge: Bus from Lijiang Long distance station (¥40-60)
  • Shuhe Ancient Town: Bus #6 or Didi (¥20-30)
  • Baisha Village: Bus #6 or cycle (12km from old town)

⚠️ Important Tips

  • Old Town Entry: You may need to show ticket at some entrances
  • Altitude: At 2,400m, take it easy for first day. Jade Dragon is higher.
  • Oxygen: Available at convenience stores. ¥10-20 for canned oxygen.
  • Luggage: Wheeled bags difficult on cobblestones. Light packing recommended.
