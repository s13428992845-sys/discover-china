Xining

The high-altitude capital — gateway to the Tibetan Plateau, famous for Kumbum Monastery and multicultural heritage.

Home / Qinghai Province / Xining

City Overview

Xining sits at 2,200m on the edge of the Tibetan Plateau. It's China's most ethnically diverse provincial capital, with Tibetan, Hui Muslim, Mongu, and Han cultures coexisting. The city is the gateway to Qinghai Lake and the Tibetan regions.

📍 Location

Eastern Qinghai, in the Huangshui River valley at 2,200m elevation. Gateway to the Tibetan Plateau.

🕌 Multi-Cultural

Buddhist temples and Muslim mosques side by side. Home to Tibetan, Hui, Mongu, and Han communities.

🏛️ Kumbum Monastery

One of Tibetan Buddhism's six major monasteries — birthplace of Tsongkhapa, founder of the Gelug school.

🌡️ Altitude

At 2,200m — mild altitude effects possible. Good acclimatization point before higher destinations.

Top Attractions

🏛️ Kumbum Monastery

Founded 1583 — ornate halls, the Great Pagoda with 100,000 Buddha images, and butter sculpture art.

🍜 Food Streets

Xining's Muslim Quarter has incredible lamb skewers, hand-pulled noodles, and yak yogurt.

🏛️ Dongguan Mosque

One of China's largest mosques — Friday prayers draw 10,000+ worshippers. Impressive architecture.

🛍️ Tibetan Markets

Browse Tibetan prayer wheels, singing bowls, and thangkas in the markets around the city center.

Best Time to Visit Xining

🌸 Spring (Mar-May)

Warming up. Occasional sandstorms. Good for monastery visits with fewer crowds.

☀️ Summer (Jun-Aug)

Best season. Pleasant 15-25°C. Ideal base for Qinghai Lake and the plateau.

🍂 Autumn (Sep-Nov)

Cool and dry. Beautiful clear skies. Good for acclimatization before higher travel.

❄️ Winter (Dec-Feb)

Cold (-15 to 0°C). Few tourists. Lower prices but limited outdoor activities.

Xining Travel Tips

⚠️ Important Reminders

  • Acclimatize: Spend 1-2 days in Xining before heading to higher areas (Qinghai Lake at 3,200m). Take it easy.
  • Muslim Quarter: The area around Dongguan Mosque has the best local food. Try yak meat dishes and lamb skewers.
  • Kumbum Tips: Allow 3 hours. The butter sculpture exhibition hall is unique — colorful yak butter sculptures of Buddhist scenes.
  • Thursday Market: The Thursday market near the East Gate has the best selection of Tibetan and Muslim crafts.
