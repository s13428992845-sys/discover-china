Xijiang Miao Village

The world's largest Miao ethnic village — over 1,000 wooden stilt houses cascading down emerald mountain slopes in Guizhou.

Home / Guizhou / Xijiang

📍 What to Experience

The Thousand-Household Village

Walk through the world's largest Miao village. Sunrise and sunset views from the observation deck show the entire valley lit up.

Miao Silver & Embroidery

Watch artisans craft intricate Miao silver jewelry and colorful embroidered costumes — some pieces take months to complete.

Long-Table Banquet (长桌宴)

Join a traditional Miao feast served on a single continuous table winding through the village streets.

🎫 Tickets

  • Admission: ¥90 (includes shuttle bus)
  • Sightseeing car: ¥5 per ride (observation deck)
  • Long-Table Banquet: ¥60-80 per person

💡 Travel Tips

  • Best visit during Miao New Year (October/November) or Sister's Meal Festival (April)
  • Stay overnight in a stilt house guesthouse for the full experience
  • Take the shuttle to the top observation deck at sunset for spectacular photos
  • From Guiyang: ~3 hours by bus or car
