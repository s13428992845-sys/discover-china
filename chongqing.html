Chongqing

Known as the "Mountain City" and "Fog City" — experience the cyberpunk Hongyadong skyline, world-famous hotpot, and gateway to the legendary Three Gorges cruise.

Home / Provinces / Chongqing

Province Overview

Chongqing is a massive municipality in Southwest China, built on steep hills at the confluence of the Yangtze and Jialing rivers. Famous for its cyberpunk skyline, fiery hotpot, and as the gateway to the Three Gorges. With ~32 million people, it's China's most populous city.

📍 Location

Southwest China, at the upper reaches of the Yangtze River. Gateway to the Three Gorges.

👥 Population

Approximately 32 million (municipality), making it China's most populous city.

🌡️ Climate

Hot and humid summers, mild winters. Known as one of China's "furnace cities". Best visited Oct-Apr.

🏔️ Terrain

Extremely mountainous — buildings on steep hills. Rivers, gorges, and karst formations.

🍜 Cuisine

Famous for numbing-spicy (麻辣) hotpot, xiaomian noodles, and incredible street food.

✈️ Major Airport

Chongqing Jiangbei International Airport (CKG) — major hub with international flights.

Why Visit Chongqing?

🌃 Cyberpunk Cityscape

Hongyadong at night looks like a scene from Blade Runner. Neon lights on 11-story stilted buildings!

🍲 Hotpot Capital

The world's hotpot capital. Authentic 麻辣 (numbing-spicy) experience that defines the city.

🚢 Three Gorges Gateway

Starting point for the legendary Yangtze River Three Gorges cruise. A journey of a lifetime!

🏗️ 8D Transportation

Light rail through buildings, roads stacking on top of each other. An urban planning marvel!

🏘️ Ciqikou Ancient Town

1,000-year-old trading port with Ming-Qing architecture, tea houses, and local snacks.

🌄 Wulong Karst

UNESCO World Heritage site with natural bridges, caves, and "Transformers 4" filming location.

Best Time to Visit

🌸 Spring (March - May)

Warm and pleasant, perfect for sightseeing and outdoor activities. Ideal weather for exploring.

☀️ Summer (June - August)

Extremely hot (40°C+). Avoid if possible, but cheaper accommodations and fewer crowds.

🍂 Autumn (September - November)

Best season! Comfortable temperatures, clear skies, ideal for Three Gorges cruise.

❄️ Winter (December - February)

Cool and foggy. Perfect for hotpot! Fewer tourists and lower prices.

Getting Around Chongqing

✈️ By Air

  • Major Airport: Chongqing Jiangbei (CKG), major hub with domestic and international flights
  • Connections: Direct flights to Beijing, Shanghai, Chengdu, Hong Kong, and more
  • Airport Metro: Line 10 connects airport to city center

🚄 By Train

  • High-Speed Rail: To Chengdu (1hr), Guiyang, Wuhan, Shanghai. Very convenient!
  • Three Gorges Cruises: Starting point for legendary Yangtze River cruises
  • Metro: 12+ lines covering major attractions

🚇 Metro / Light Rail

  • Building Train: Line 2 passes THROUGH a residential building — a must-see!
  • Extensive Network: 12+ lines covering most attractions
  • Scenic Rides: Lines along cliffs and rivers offer great views

🚗 Taxi / Didi

  • Affordable: Taxis and Didi are very reasonably priced
  • Traffic: Can be heavy during rush hours
  • GPS Challenge: GPS often struggles with the 3D road system

Chongqing Travel Tips

⚠️ Important Reminders

  • Prepare for Spice: Even "non-spicy" dishes may contain chili. Say "不要辣" (bù yào là) for no spice.
  • Comfortable Shoes: The city is extremely hilly with stairs everywhere. Wear sturdy footwear!
  • Fog is Common: Don't worry if mornings are grey — it often clears by afternoon.
  • Hongyadong Timing: Best at dusk/night — go around 6-7pm for the full neon effect.
  • Yangtze Cable Car: Offers amazing views — cross the river in style at Chaotianmen!
  • Book Three Gorges Cruises: They book up in advance — reserve at least 2 weeks ahead.
  • Summer Heat: Temperatures can exceed 40°C — stay hydrated and plan indoor activities.
  • Direct Communication: Chongqing people are direct and friendly — don't be offended by their straightforward style.
