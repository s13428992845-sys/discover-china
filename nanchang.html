Nanchang

The heroic capital — Tengwang Pavilion, revolutionary history, and the starting point of the People's Liberation Army.

Home / Jiangxi Province / Nanchang

City Overview

Nanchang is the capital of Jiangxi province — a city with deep revolutionary significance as the birthplace of the People's Liberation Army. It also boasts the famous Tengwang Pavilion, one of the three great towers of southern China.

📍 Location

Northern Jiangxi, on the Gan River near Poyang Lake. HSR hub connecting east-west China.

🏛️ Revolutionary History

The 1927 Nanchang Uprising — birthplace of the PLA. August 1st is Army Day, celebrated here.

🏯 Tengwang Pavilion

One of three great towers of Jiangnan — 1,300 years of literary fame. Rebuilt 29 times.

🌊 Poyang Lake

China's largest freshwater lake — a critical wetland for migratory birds, especially Siberian cranes.

Top Attractions

🏯 Tengwang Pavilion

The iconic tower — climb for views of the Gan River. Beautiful at sunset and when lit at night.

🏛️ August 1st Uprising HQ

The original headquarters of the 1927 uprising — now a museum documenting the birth of the PLA.

🌊 Poyang Lake

China's largest freshwater lake — bird watching in winter, vast waters in summer.

🏛️ Bayi Park

A park commemorating the August 1st Uprising — monuments, museums, and peaceful gardens.

Best Time to Visit Nanchang

🌸 Spring (Mar-May)

Pleasant warming. Good for outdoor activities. Migratory birds departing from Poyang Lake.

☀️ Summer (Jun-Aug)

Hot and humid (35°C+). Best for lake activities and indoor attractions.

🍂 Autumn (Sep-Nov)

Best season. Cool, clear, comfortable. Migratory birds start arriving at Poyang Lake.

❄️ Winter (Dec-Feb)

Cool (2-8°C). Peak bird watching at Poyang Lake — 700,000+ migratory birds.

Nanchang Travel Tips

⚠️ Important Reminders

  • Tengwang Pavilion: Visit at sunset — the view of the Gan River with the illuminated pavilion is stunning.
  • Food: Try 南昌拌粉 (Nanchang mixed rice noodles) — the city's signature breakfast dish.
  • Bird Watching: Poyang Lake in December-January hosts the world's largest concentration of Siberian cranes.
  • Day Trip: Lushan Mountain is only 1.5 hours from Nanchang — a perfect day trip.
